OUR SERVICES

MINING & MINERAL DEVELOPMENT

OpalGeo Solutions Ltd brings together a team of experts in Mineral Development & Rock Evaluation to undertake Geologic mapping, Geophysical & Geochemical surveys with an objective of appraising Mineral Resources and evaluating their economic potential. We conduct surveys to prospect and explore for minerals through field mapping, Sampling (soil/stream/grab/rock/bedrock), trenching, coring, and assaying. We undertake reserve appraisals, plan, design & manage both Surface & Underground mines. Our team is experienced in exploring precious metals like Gold, Copper, Tin, Tantalite; Base metals and Rocks such as Granites, Marble, Quartzites, and Industrial minerals like Sand, Clay, limestone. We also guide our clients during licence application and compliance.

At OpalGeo Solutions Ltd, we are also committed to building capacity of both National and Foreign Entities and Individuals in oil and gas development through Training & Training Materials & Tools, Industry Softwares and other Short Courses for them to remain competitive in the market.
