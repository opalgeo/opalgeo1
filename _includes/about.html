ABOUT US

OPALGEO Solutions Ltd is the leading Consultancy Partner when it comes to Sustainably Harnessing Natural Resources through Geosciences, Engineering and Environmental Services & Products.

Established in 2019, the Company has become a Renowned Hub of Seasoned Geologists, Geoscientists, Environmental Gurus, Civil & Mechanical Engineers, Health & Safety Experts as well as Energy Economists in the Great-Lakes Region. Since its incorporation, OpalGeo solutions Ltd has remained committed to providing High-quality, Eco-friendly, Cost-effective and Timebound services to Individuals, Institutions, Organisations, Companies, NGOs & The Governments.

For all our projects, Occupational and Environmental Health & Safety are at the forefront and we are committed to cultivating the Safety culture across our Operations, Internal staff and any third parties on the projects. At OpalGeo Solutions, all our projects go through Impact Assessments before implementation because Safety is how we do business!

OpalGeo Solutions Ltd. aims to provide Worldclass Services using Multi-disciplinary Contemporary approaches. We continually invest in our staff and technical facilities so we can combine this with our extensive global experience and pass this on to our customers as a service of the highest quality. We stay committed to Building lasting Capacity: Educating and Training recent Graduates, and the industry at large, through Technical contributions to Research Projects and informative Publications is priority for us.

In the nutshell, we remain committed to collaboratively address Energy Poverty, ensure Energy Diversification, yet assure Energy Sustainability on the continent.
